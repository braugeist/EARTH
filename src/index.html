About EARTH

EARTH is a 3D visualization of planet Earth that features 812 ownable Earth tiles. Each tile is represented by an ERC-721 token on Ethereum and can display a custom message.

Structure: The 812 tiles consist of 800 hexagons and 12 pentagons.

Customize: You can display a custom message along with your tile.

Trade: Mint your tiles here and trade them at a marketplace such as OpenSea!
